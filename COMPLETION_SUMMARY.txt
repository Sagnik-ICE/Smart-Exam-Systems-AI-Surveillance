## 🎬 What Was Fixed

### The Issue
- Webcam video was positioned in the normal document flow
- Scrolling through exam questions would move the camera out of view
- Small video size (180×120px) was hard to see

### The Solution
✅ **Floating Webcam Widget** (position: fixed)
- Stays in top-right corner while scrolling
- Larger size (220×165px desktop, 160×120px mobile)
- Professional appearance with teal border
- Black background for better video visibility
- z-index 9999 ensures always visible
